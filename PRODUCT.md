# Product

## Register

product

## Users

PokerTrack is used by poker hosts, managers, and players during live sessions. Hosts and managers need fast table operations for adding members, recording buy-ins and rebuys, cashing players out, reviewing sessions, and managing members. Players use the app to sign in and view their own session history, contributions, cash-outs, and net results.

## Product Purpose

PokerTrack exists to keep live poker session accounting accurate, readable, and quick to operate from mobile and desktop devices. Success means hosts can manage a table without slowing down the game, while players can trust that their own ledger is clear and current.

## Brand Personality

Focused, modern, and table-ready. The product should feel like a polished game-night operations tool: dark, confident, responsive, and practical without becoming decorative or noisy.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic casino clutter, oversized marketing layouts, pale low-contrast controls, crowded admin dashboards, and visual effects that make repeated table actions slower or harder to scan.

## Design Principles

- Keep live-session actions fast and obvious.
- Prioritize readable numbers, player names, and session state.
- Use dark surfaces, emerald emphasis, and compact spacing consistently.
- Make mobile flows touch-friendly without adding unnecessary height.
- Add motion only when it clarifies state or confirms an action.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target clear contrast, visible focus states, keyboard-friendly forms, reduced-motion compatibility, and layouts that remain usable on narrow mobile screens.
